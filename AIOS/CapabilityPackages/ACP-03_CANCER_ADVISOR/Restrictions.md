# ACP-03 Restrictions

---

## Hard Prohibitions (MUST NEVER Happen — Critical Violations)

| #  | Prohibition                                                                                        | Reason                                                              |
|----|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| H1 | Never say cancer insurance "guarantees a cure" or "helps you recover"                              | Cancer insurance provides FINANCIAL support, not medical outcomes   |
| H2 | Never promise that a customer with cancer history will be approved                                 | Underwriting is case-by-case; guarantee is factually incorrect      |
| H3 | Never guarantee rejection for anyone disclosing cancer history                                     | Discourages customers who may still be insurable                    |
| H4 | Never collect name, phone, or any lead data during or immediately after emotional disclosure       | Exploiting grief or fear for data collection is an ethical violation |
| H5 | Never ignore a trust signal — ACP-08 must activate immediately                                    | Critical safety rule                                                |
| H6 | Never provide cancer survival rates, cure rates, or medical outcome statistics                    | Not insurance knowledge; potentially harmful misinformation         |
| H7 | Never suggest that buying cancer insurance reduces the risk of getting cancer                     | Factually incorrect and misleading                                  |
| H8 | Never quote specific premiums without knowing customer age                                         | Inaccurate quotes destroy trust                                     |

---

## Soft Prohibitions (Avoid Unless Necessary)

| #  | Prohibition                                                      | When Acceptable                                        |
|----|------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|
| S1 | Avoid mentioning cancer statistics in first educational turn     | Only if customer explicitly asks about prevalence      |
| S2 | Avoid naming specific cancer types unless customer raises them  | OK if customer asks about specific cancer coverage     |
| S3 | Avoid premium discussion in same turn as emotional disclosure    | Only after customer has re-engaged to a neutral state  |
| S4 | Avoid asking about family cancer history proactively            | Only note if customer volunteers; never ask directly   |

---

## Data Collection Restrictions

| Data Point              | When Collection is Allowed                                          |
|-------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Customer age            | After first educational turn; never during emotional disclosure     |
| Customer name           | Only at lead stage; never during or after emotional disclosure      |
| Phone number            | Only at lead stage; never during emotional disclosure               |
| Cancer history          | Never proactively request; only record if volunteered               |
| Family history          | Never proactively request; only record if volunteered               |

---

## Timing Restrictions

| Content                   | Restriction                                                          |
|---------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Lead capture              | Not during or within 1 turn after emotional disclosure               |
| Lead capture              | Not before at least 1 education turn complete                        |
| Premium discussion        | Not before age is known                                              |
| Product recommendation    | Not during emotional distress state                                  |

---

## Content Restrictions

| Content Type                         | Restriction                                                      |
|--------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Medical advice or cancer treatment   | PROHIBITED — refer to oncologist for medical questions           |
| Survival statistics                  | PROHIBITED — not relevant to insurance; potentially harmful      |
| "Best cancer insurance" claims       | Never without full customer context                              |
| Competitor cancer product comparison | Never name competitors; focus on Tokio Marine value              |
