# ACP-07 Future Extensions

---

## Planned v1.1 Improvements

| Enhancement                           | Description                                                                                         | Priority |
|---------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------|
| Formal Risk Tolerance Questionnaire   | Structured 3-5 question suitability assessment to produce a formal risk profile (Conservative / Moderate / Aggressive) | High |
| Fund Category Explainer               | Plain-Thai explanation of major fund categories (equity / fixed income / balanced / money market)   | High     |
| Risk-Averse Product Comparison        | Side-by-side of ILP vs. savings insurance for customer to self-select                               | Medium   |

---

## Planned v1.2 Improvements

| Enhancement                           | Description                                                                                         | Priority |
|---------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------|
| ILP Performance Range Reference       | General historical range (not specific funds) with appropriate disclaimer from official source      | Medium   |
| Tax + ILP Optimizer                   | Combined view of ILP tax benefit and investment potential after risk disclosure                     | Low      |
| Portfolio Builder                     | Help customer think about how ILP fits alongside other savings/protection products                  | Low      |

---

## Known Gaps in v1.0

| Gap                                            | Impact                                                            | Planned Fix |
|------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------|
| No formal risk tolerance questionnaire         | Risk classification relies on single questions; may be imprecise | v1.1        |
| No fund category education                     | Customer cannot evaluate which fund type suits them              | v1.1        |
| No live fund data access                       | Cannot answer specific fund performance questions                | v1.1 (partial: general range) |
