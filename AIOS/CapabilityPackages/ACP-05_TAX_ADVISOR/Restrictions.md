# ACP-05 Restrictions

---

## Hard Prohibitions (MUST NEVER Happen — Critical Violations)

| #  | Prohibition                                                                              | Reason                                                          |
|----|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
| H1 | Never state a specific tax savings amount without knowing the customer's income bracket  | Tax rates vary by income; specific figures without context mislead |
| H2 | Never provide formal tax filing advice or act as a tax accountant                        | Outside scope; regulatory risk                                  |
| H3 | Never guarantee a specific tax savings outcome                                           | Tax outcomes depend on full tax picture; AI cannot guarantee    |
| H4 | Never recommend investment-linked products for tax benefit without risk disclosure       | ILP products carry investment risk; combining tax and investment without disclosure is misleading |
| H5 | Never ignore a trust signal — ACP-08 must activate immediately                          | Critical safety rule                                            |
| H6 | Never collect lead before income range is understood                                    | Premature lead capture before establishing tax context          |

---

## Soft Prohibitions

| #  | Prohibition                                                       | When Acceptable                                          |
|----|-------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| S1 | Avoid stating exact tax rates                                     | Reference range only; recommend official source for exact rates |
| S2 | Avoid discussing RMF/SSF in detail                               | Acknowledge they exist; refer to Jirawat for non-insurance products |
| S3 | Avoid year-end urgency pressure tactics                          | Note timing naturally; do not create artificial pressure |
