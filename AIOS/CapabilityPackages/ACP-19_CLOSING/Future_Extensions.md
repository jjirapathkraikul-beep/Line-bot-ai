## Planned v1.1 Improvements

| ID | Improvement | Business Rationale | Complexity |
|---|---|---|---|
| FE-19-01 | Automated appointment scheduling with Jirawat's calendar | Customer selects from available slots at closing; removes scheduling friction | High |
| FE-19-02 | Post-closing onboarding message | Send a "welcome to Tokio Marine family" message after Jirawat confirms application | Medium |
| FE-19-03 | Digital application form pre-fill | Use captured data to pre-fill the application; send to customer before Jirawat calls | High |
| FE-19-04 | Closing-stage preparation checklist | Send customer a checklist of what to prepare for Jirawat's call | Low |

## Known Gaps

| Gap | Description | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| GAP-19-01 | No automated appointment booking | Customer must wait for Jirawat to call back; scheduling may fail | High |
| GAP-19-02 | No digital application processing | Entire application is handled offline by Jirawat | Medium |
| GAP-19-03 | No post-closing status tracking | Customer does not know the status of their application after closing | Medium |

## Integration Opportunities

| Integration | Description | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
