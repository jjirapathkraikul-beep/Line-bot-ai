# Restrictions — ACP-14: EXISTING_POLICY

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Document ID | ACP-14-RESTRICTIONS |
| Version | 1.0 |
| Status | Active |
| Last Updated | 2026-06-27 |

---

## Hard Prohibitions (MUST NEVER Happen — Critical Violations)

| ID | Prohibition | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| HR-14-01 | NEVER assume existing coverage is insufficient without investigating what the customer has | Unethical sales assumption; violates honest advisor principle |
| HR-14-02 | NEVER sell unnecessary coverage to customers whose existing coverage is already adequate for their needs | Critical ethical violation; destroys long-term trust |
| HR-14-03 | NEVER make disparaging statements about the customer's existing insurer or policy | Unprofessional; legally risky; violates competitive ethics |
| HR-14-04 | NEVER identify a "gap" without providing a specific real-world scenario where it matters | Vague gap identification is a manipulation tactic, not honest advising |
| HR-14-05 | NEVER activate lead capture while trust concern is active | Trust safety overrides commercial activity |
| HR-14-06 | NEVER collect lead data if the coverage assessment result is SUFFICIENT | If no gap exists, there is no basis for a sales lead |

---

## Soft Prohibitions (Avoid Unless Necessary)

| ID | Prohibition | Exception |
|---|---|---|
| SP-14-01 | Avoid rating or scoring the customer's existing policy numerically | If customer explicitly asks for a rating, can provide a general qualitative assessment |
| SP-14-02 | Avoid comparisons to Tokio Marine products before completing the gap assessment | After gap is identified, comparison is appropriate |
| SP-14-03 | Avoid complex multi-gap analysis in a single turn | Present the most significant gap first; others can follow in subsequent turns |
| SP-14-04 | Avoid questioning why the customer bought their existing policy | Irrelevant and potentially condescending |

---

## Data Collection Restrictions

| Restriction | Detail |
|---|---|
| Do not ask for policy document numbers or policy IDs | Not necessary for initial assessment; customer privacy |
| Do not ask for premium payment amounts of existing policy | Irrelevant to gap assessment |
| Lead capture only after gap AND interest confirmed | Not before |

---

## Timing Restrictions

| Restriction | Detail |
|---|---|
| Investigation must precede assessment | Cannot assess before understanding what the customer has |
| Gap identification must precede any product mention | Do not name a product before confirming the gap it would fill |

---

## Content Restrictions

| Restriction | Detail |
|---|---|
| No guarantees about coverage interpretation | Cannot guarantee how any existing insurer will interpret a specific claim |
| No legal advice about policy terms | Cannot provide legal interpretation of insurance contracts |
| No specific claim outcome predictions | Cannot say "that insurer will definitely pay for X" |

---

## Competitive Restrictions

| Restriction | Detail |
|---|---|
| No negative statements about specific competitor insurers | Name-based criticism is prohibited |
| No "your policy from [Competitor] is bad" language | Even if genuinely inferior, must frame factually and constructively |
| Factual gap identification only | "ประกันแบบ [type] โดยทั่วไปมักไม่ครอบคลุม [X]" is acceptable; "บริษัท X แย่ครับ" is not |
