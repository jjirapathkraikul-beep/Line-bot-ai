# 1. Buying Signal

### Metadata
- Version: 1.0
- Effective Date: 2026-06-26

## 1. Purpose
Define signals that indicate purchase intent and how they should change routing or urgency.

## 2. Scope
Verbal cues, numeric indicators (budget), and behavior (repeat questions).

## 3. Inputs
- Conversation events
- Lead scoring data

## 4. Outputs
- Route to sales or human follow-up

## 5. Dependencies
- Lead Scoring
- Trust Engine

## 6. Future Improvements
- Calibrate thresholds from production data
